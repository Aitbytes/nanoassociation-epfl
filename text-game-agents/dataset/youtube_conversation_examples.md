## Key Findings Summary

### Core Text Message Principles Found Across Transcripts:

1. **Don't spam emojis** — Use sparingly to emphasize jokes, not as decoration
2. **Don't double text** — Shows neediness; let her respond at her pace
3. **Text with purpose** — Either set up a date, get on phone/FaceTime, or reference something specific
4. **Match response energy** — Don't reply faster than she does
5. **No paragraphs** — Keep texts short; voice messages are better for long messages
6. **Don't ask too many questions** — Make statements; engage conversationally
7. **Be direct about intentions** — Ask for the date within first 1-3 messages
8. **Use assumption statements** — "You look like you know where to find the best X in town"
9. **Personalize first messages** — Reference something specific from her profile
